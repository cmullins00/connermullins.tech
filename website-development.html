E-Commerce Platform
Development

In collaboration with two other students, I developed an e-commerce website that integrates a search system akin to Best Buy and enables users and businesses to list and purchase goods and services similar to eBay. The site features vendor listings for tech products and services, with various internet service options and pricing. I managed databases for both products and user/vendor accounts, overseeing the implementation of advanced search and filtering capabilities, such as price range, brand, and screen size. Additionally, I designed a feature to display products just outside the specified price range and formatted the product display for each page. The website was built using PHP, HTML, CSS, and MySQL, and included a tool for users to estimate their bandwidth needs based on device requirements.
